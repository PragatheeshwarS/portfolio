Terra Trooper

The Terra Trooper is a versatile Hexapod robot equipped with six legs, designed specifically for navigating challenging terrain where human access is limited. Its primary application lies in disaster management scenarios, where rescue operations often face uncertainty about the conditions below. However, the Terra Trooper excels in such environments, capable of traversing various terrains while autonomously detecting obstacles in its path. Utilizing advanced algorithms like Rapidly Exploring Random Trees (RRT), it efficiently maps the quickest route from its starting point to the destination. For precise object detection, it integrates YOLO-v5 technology, fine-tuned with customized input parameters to achieve an outstanding accuracy rate of 99%. Moreover, the Terra Trooper boasts resilience against damage through its innovative tripod gait algorithm. Even with three legs alternately impaired, it retains the ability to travel effectively, ensuring continued operation under challenging conditions.
